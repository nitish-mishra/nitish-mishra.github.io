About Me

My name is Thomas Frank, and I’m a senior at Iowa State University studying Management Information Systems.

I’ve done a lot of stuff, but I’m probably best known for being the founder of  College Info Geek, one of the biggest student-focused blogs on the Internet. I also design websites and write for other blogs.

In the past, I’ve been an RA, did an IT internship at Principal Financial Group, worked as a tech support dude and virus removal expert, helped thousands of new students start their college careers at Iowa State, tutored college students while still in high school, and spent more than a few hours trudging through corn fields.

I’m obsessed with entrepreneurship, human potential, and DDR. When I grow up, I want to write, speak, and teach people how to shoot for the stars.

This site is my personal website, which is also an aggregator for all my web design work, guest posts, and all the raving press about me. Oh, and there’s some sort of blog, but it’s on Tumblr now. You can also hire me to do whatever crap your heart desires, or stalk me.

People I’m ridiculously inspired by: Derek Sivers, Denise Minger, Nicholas Cardot, Kelly Sutton.
